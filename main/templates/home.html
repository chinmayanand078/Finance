Backtesting Engine

The platform offers a backtesting engine, comprehensive performance analysis tools, and advanced risk management techniques for developing, testing, and optimizing trading strategies. Backtesting is vital for evaluating the performance of trading strategies using historical market data before live deployment. This process helps in understanding potential risks and rewards, optimizing strategies, and gaining confidence in their viability, enabling informed trading decisions.

Key Features

Advanced Backtesting Engine:

Perform backtesting on equities with your preferred trading strategies and risk management techniques. Evaluate strategy performance using historical market data.

CSV Market Data Support

Upload and backtest your strategies using provided CSV market data, enabling analysis on customized and specific market scenarios.

Custom Strategy Creation:

Create and test your own trading strategies directly on our platform, allowing for tailored strategy development to fit your unique trading objectives.

Risk Management:

Implement risk management techniques, such as stop-loss orders to effectively measure and control strategy risk.

Comprehensive Performance Analysis:

Conduct real-time simulations to see how your strategies perform under current market conditions, providing a realistic view of potential outcomes and analyze strategy.
